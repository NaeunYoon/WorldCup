Thank you for purchasing the Stylized_Hit_Impact_vol_1
This post describes how to enable sprite atlas. In order for the effects to work properly.

In order for the textures in the effects to load, you need to enable the sprite atlas. To do this, go to Edit/Project Settings---Editor---Sprite Packer/mode --- **** Enable.
Then click on Play Mode.

There are 4 demo scenes in the project depending on the rendering pipeline:
Demo_scene_All_render - universal scene. For all rendering pipelines. 
Demo_scene_built-in (without post-processing) 
Demo_scene_URP - used for recording previews 
Demo_scene_HDRP 
The demo scenes themselves may differ due to differences in rendering pipelines
